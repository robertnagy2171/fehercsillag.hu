Welcome to our website!

Wander with us about the world of the Union of Hungarian Women (UHW), which, we believe, is the basis of the society and sustainability of the future.
The aim of the UHW is to facilitate the dialogue between legislation, the governmental, civil, business and academic spheres to ensure that the economic, social, employment policy, educational, environmental, health preservation and consumer protection decisions serve the interests of women and families.
In our urbanizing world, more and more women are becoming lonely and so their support is a priority issue for the Union of Hungarian Women.

The UHW believes in the unity of men and women
– we refuse the extremities of feminist movements!

As a result of the cooperation between the two sexes, more balanced families and jobs can be established. Instead of rivalry, mutual help can enter the homes and workplaces alike. The UHW professes that the tranquillity and peace of the family is the basis of social stability and a key to the success of the future.
Women – also as mothers of families – play a decisive role in preserving time-honoured family values and traditions as well as in passing them on to future generations.
Without the involvement and active support of women the Union of Hungarian Women believes that the achievement of such objectives as the preservation of the maintaining and retaining capacity of rural areas, community building, high quality production, environmental awareness, food safety, and supporting the cause of talent nurturing is inconceivable.
